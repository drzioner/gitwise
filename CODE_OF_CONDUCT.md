# Code of Conduct

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [Español](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.es.md)

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and respectful environment for
everyone participating in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and outcomes, not people.
- Accept and apply useful feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that could reasonably make participation unsafe.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
discussions, and other official communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to
`drzioner@gmail.com` with the subject `gitwise conduct report`.

We will review reports promptly and handle them with care and confidentiality.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close conversations, or block contributors for
behavior that violates this policy.
